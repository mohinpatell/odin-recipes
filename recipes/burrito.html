Burrito Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for a refried bean and cheese burrito. Burritos are good, and you should try to make it if you can.

Ingredients for Burrito

  • Refried Beans
  • Tortilla
  • Shredded Cheese

Steps to make Burrito

  1. Spread refried beans on a tortilla.
  2. Sprinkle shredded cheese over the refried beans.
  3. Roll up the tortilla.
