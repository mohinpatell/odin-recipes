Tomato Soup Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for tomato soup. Tomato soup is good, and you should try to make it if you can.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can stewed tomatoes
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large saucepan over medium heat. Saute onion in butter until tender.
  2. Stir in tomatoes, chicken broth, sugar, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, and cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Puree tomato mixture until smooth.
