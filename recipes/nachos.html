Nachos Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for nachos. Nachos are good, and you should try to make it if you can.

Ingredients for Nachos

  • Refried Beans
  • Tortilla Chips
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Chopped Tomatoes
  • Chopped Onions
  • Chopped Jalapenos
  • Sour Cream
  • Guacamole

Steps to make Nachos

  1. Spread tortilla chips on a baking sheet.
  2. Spoon refried beans over the tortilla chips.
  3. Sprinkle shredded cheese over the tortilla chips and beans.
  4. Place baking sheet in oven and bake at 350 degrees for 10 minutes.
  5. Remove baking sheet from oven and sprinkle chopped tomatoes, onions, and jalapenos over the nachos.
  6. Top with sour cream and guacamole.
